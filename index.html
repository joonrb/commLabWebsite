Why Hamad Milkshake?

The taste and freshness of an oasis in your hands! Hamad Milkshake, first established in 1988, was opened in under principle: provide the freshest sweetness to our customers. Our classic milkshake, the Hamad Milkshake, has been loved for the past 34 years and is still the best selling milkshake in the world. We provide the freshest, most delicious milkshakes all around the world at our Hamad Milkshake branches.

To advertise our famous Hamad Milkshake, we produced an advertisement of the Hamad Milkshake, tasting so delicious that it changed a man's life into a Hamad himself. Taste our perfection of a milkshake and become a Hamad yourself! Our milkshakes are so delicious, it will turn you into a Hamad!
